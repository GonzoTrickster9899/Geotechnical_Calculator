About Us and Project

A comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline geotechnical engineering calculations for academic excellence and professional development.

Academic Project Civil Engineering Bicol University

Our Mission

To provide civil engineering students and professionals with accessible, accurate, and user-friendly geotechnical calculation tools that enhance learning, improve productivity, and support evidence-based engineering decisions.

Our Vision

To become the leading educational platform for geotechnical engineering calculations, fostering innovation in civil engineering education and contributing to the development of competent engineering professionals.

Platform Features

Comprehensive tools designed for modern geotechnical engineering

Bearing Capacity Analysis

Advanced calculations based on Terzaghi's bearing capacity theory for shallow foundation design.

Foundation Design

Comprehensive tools for both shallow and deep foundation analysis and design optimization.
